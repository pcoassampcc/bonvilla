About Us

Welcome to Bonvilla Retreat, where nature meets luxury. Nestled amidst serene landscapes, we offer an escape from the hustle of city life. Our eco-friendly cottages, organic cuisines, and adventure activities ensure a rejuvenating stay for all. Established in 2010, we have been providing unforgettable experiences to travelers seeking tranquility and connection with nature.

Our retreat spans over 20 acres of lush greenery, featuring organic farms, nature trails, and breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. We are committed to sustainable tourism and preserving the natural beauty of our location.
